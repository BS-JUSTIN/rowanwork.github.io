Overseas

Short Description:

Inside the red wall, there is the style of civilisation thousands of miles away. The foreign cultural relics treasured by the Forbidden City come across the mountains and seas, with exotic ingenuity and customs. They are the old collections of the court, national gifts, and precious marks on the silk road and the shipping route. The utensils are exquisite and unique, integrating the beauty of craft, the wisdom of science and technology and the charm of culture. Every tool and every thing is a gentle witness to the dialogue of civilisation, recording the encounter between the East and the West, tradition and exotic places, and quietly telling the good words of communication across time and space in the Forbidden City....

Product Tags

The Palace Museum has about 13,000 foreign cultural relics,mainly the old collection of the Qing Palace,mostly donated,gifted and collected by countries in the 18th and 19th centuries,covering Britain, France, Germany, Japan, Korea and many other countries.The category is the most characteristic of European watches, as well as Western scientific and technological instruments, enamels, glassware, Japanese lacquerware, Korean stationery,etc which  are important physical evidences of Sino-foreign exchanges in the Ming and Qing Dynasties and Western learning from the East.Cultural relics have been exhibited in the Clock and Watch Museum and Treasure Museum of the Forbidden City for a long time. Xiamen Gulangyu has set up a foreign cultural relics branch museum,focussing on the display of exotic crafts and court utensils which have both artistic,scientific and technological and historical values.

1.Copper tire painting enamel rattan pattern seven-drawer cabinet box

  Copper tire painting enamel box, imitation Chinese-style standing cabinet, with Ruyi beam, with seven drawers inside. Light blue enamel, painted with purple and green vine, fresh and elegant. Painting enamel originated in Europe and was introduced to the court by Kangxi in the Qing Dynasty. This vessel combines Western craftsmanship with oriental flower and bird patterns.

2.Western porcelain colourful print with gold bottle

   Place of origin and naming: It belongs to the so-called "foreign porcelain" in the Qing Palace archives, that is, exquisite porcelain fired in modern Japan. This one is one of the pairs' collection.

   Decoration features: The bottle body is painted with colourful glaze iris (called "iris" in Japan).The flowers are full, purple, blue and white colours contrast with each other, and the leaves are painted with ink green materials, which is full of vitality. The mouth and contour of the bottle are decorated with gold colour to show luxury. The overall painting style is realistic and delicate, which not only retains the imitation of Chinese colourful porcelain, but also shows the vivid colour and craft characteristics of Japanese Meiji porcelain.

3.Western porcelain green ground sticky flower six-edged double-eared bottle

   Place of origin and technology: Western porcelain fired in modern Japan is also a pair collection. Its biggest feature is the adoption of "sticky flower"technology also known as "stacking plastic"

   Decorative features: the bottle body is hexagonal, and the whole body is covered with dark green glaze. The bamboo branches and bamboo leaves of the bottle are not drawn, but moulded with porcelain clay and pasted on the surface of the vessel,and then glazed and fired, which has a strong sense of three-dimensionality. The bamboo leaves are outlined in golden colour, and the bamboo joints are distinct, supplemented by plum blossoms, forming the traditional auspicious meaning of "plum and bamboo double clear".It is a masterpiece of Japanese craftsmen to interpret traditional Chinese themes with     innovative technology.

4.Carved lacquer inlaid Baibao double dragon ear large bottle

   Foreign classified cultural relics that entered the Qing Palace after being made in China. Red and black two-colour carved lacquer, decorated with cloud dragon patterns, and the double dragon ears on the neck are magnificent. The belly of the bottle is inlaid with snails, ivory, jade, etc., forming a picture of cranes and deer in the same spring. The carving is deep and the inlay is gorgeous.

5.Art Nouveau-style painted character pattern bottle

  European Art Nouveau movement works. The shape is twisted and smooth, with hollow ears, pale yellow and green glaze, painting Western pastoral characters. The design is inspired by natural vines and flowers, emphasising the beauty of curves. It is the display porcelain of European art innovation at the end of the 19th century.

6. Western porcelain gold-painted flower pattern boat-shaped wash

    European porcelain, imitation of Western-style boat-shaped fruit plate. Curved body, wavy mouth edge, drawing gold scroll grass pattern handle. The ground is covered with golden colours, and Western roses are painted in the light. The colour is delicate and the style is gorgeous. It is a Western handicraft that entered the Qing Palace in the middle and late Qing Dynasty.
