ABOUT

This platform focusses on the two world-class cultural heritages of the Forbidden City and Dunhuang. With digital technology as the carrier,it is committed

to the protection,research and innovative dissemination of China's excellent traditional culture.

Through high-definition digital images, non-heritage skills records, artistic interpretation and immersive experience, we let thousand-year muals, court cultural relics and non-heritage skills cross time and space and approach every user.

Protect civilisation with science and technology, revitalise tradition with creativity, and revitalise oriental aesthetics in the digital age.
